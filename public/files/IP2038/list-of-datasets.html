B List of Datasets

This document provides a list of some of the most commonly used databases capturing key political and economic trends at the domestic and international level.

B.1 Data Warehouses

  • Our World in Data
  • Gapminder
  • The International Political Economy Data Resource

B.2 Political Institutions and Political Regimes

  • Varieties of Democracy
  • Freedom House - Freedom in the World Report
  • Polity Project
  • Inter-American Development Bank - The Database of Political Institutions 2017
  • Cheibub, Gandhi, and Vreeland (2010) “Data for ’Democracy and Dictatorship Revisited”
  • Barbara Geddes, Joseph Wright, and Erica Frantz. 2014. Autocratic Regime Data.
  • Axel Hadenius, Jan Teorell, & Michael Wahman, Authoritarian Regimes Dataset (ARD)
  • Marquez, Democracy Data

B.3 Country Election

  • ElectionGuide
  • European Election Database
  • LAEDA Latin American Electoral Data
  • African Election Database
  • British General Election Constituency Results
  • Global Election Calendar
  • Global Election Database

B.4 Political Parties

  • Global Party Survey

B.5 Public Opinion Data

  • US Presidential Approval - Roper Center
  • Gallup Presidential Job Approval Center
  • The American Presidency Project - Presidential Job Approval
  • American National Election Studies
  • Executive Approval Project
  • LAPOP
  • Afrobarometer
  • Comparative Study of Election Systems

B.6 Values and Social Surveys

  • World Value Survey
  • European Social Survey
  • International Social Survey Programme

B.7 Government Macroeconomic and Financial Data

  • International Monetary Fund’s Government Finance Statistics (GFS)
  • World Bank, Open Database

B.8 Government Debt

  • World Bank’s International Debt Statistics

B.9 Inequality

  • World Wealth and Income Database (WID.world), accessible through wid-r-tool package

B.10 Foreign Direct Investment

  • International Trade Center - Investment Map
  • United Nations Conference on Trade and Development
  • Eurostat

B.11 Trade and Tariff Data

  • World Trade Organization - Trade and Tariff Data
  • International Monetary Fund Direction of Trade Statistics
  • United Nations Comtrade Labs
  • World Bank’s TC Data Initiative

B.12 Remittances

  • World Bank - Migration and Remittances Data
  • United Nations Conference on Trade and Development - Personal Remittances
  • Migration Policy Institute - Remittances Data
  • KNOMAD, Global Knowledge Partnership on Migration and Development

B.13 Corruption, Accountability and Rule of Law

  • Transparency International Corruption Perceptions Index
  • Transparency International Global Corruption Barometer
  • World Bank - Worldwide Governance Indicators (WGI)
  • World Bank - Transparency, Accountability and Corruption in the Public Sector
  • OECD - Access to Justice
  • World Justice Project Rule of Law Index

B.14 Violent Crime

  • United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime
  • World Bank - Intentional homicides
  • World Population Review - Violent Crime Rates

B.15 Organized Crime

  • United Nations Interregional Crime and Justice Research Institute
  • [United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime(https://dataunodc.un.org)

B.16 Conflict

  • Correlates of War
  • Peace Research Institute Oslo (PRIO)
  • PRIO Battledeaths Dataset
  • UCDP/PRIO Armed Conflict Dataset

B.17 Terrorism

  • Global Terrorism Dataset
